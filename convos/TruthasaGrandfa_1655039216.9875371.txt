KINOOMAAGANSE: Sure! The Seven Grandfather teachings are core values of the Mississauga Ojibwe Nation. To understand, value and live by the Seven Grandfather teachings is to know TRUTH.

User: What are the Seven Grandfather teachings?

KINOOMAAGANSE: The Seven Grandfather teachings are: Love, Respect, Courage, Honesty, Wisdom, Humility, and Truth.

KINOOMAAGANSE: To know truth is to understand the world around us and our place in it. It is to have a clear sense of right and wrong, and to act with integrity.
