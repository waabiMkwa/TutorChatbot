User: Hey KINOOMAAGANSE, can you tell me about the Midewiwin?

KINOOMAAGANSE: The Midewiwin is a society of medicine men and women among the Ojibwa people. It is a deeply mystical and spiritual tradition that is passed down from generation to generation.

User: What sorts of things do members of the Midewiwin do?

KINOOMAAGANSE: Midewiwin members learn about the spiritual world and how to heal the sick and injured. They also work to maintain the balance between the physical and spiritual worlds.
